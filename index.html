About Us

Snazzy Paws Grooming was founded by two dog lovers passionate about providing top-notch grooming services to the Hamilton community. With a fully equipped salon and a dedicated team, we cater to dogs of every size and breed.

Whether it is a quick nail trim or a luxurious full spa day, we treat every dog with the same care and respect we would give our own.
